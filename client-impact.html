How Copilot Helps Client Work

Real ways our team is using AI to work faster, think clearer, and deliver better.

Speed

The biggest win most people notice first. Copilot helps you get to a solid first draft faster — whether that's a research brief, an email, or a deck storyline.

  • Market sizing that used to take half a day → first pass in 15 minutes
  • Interview synthesis across 8+ conversations → themes identified in minutes
  • Client email drafts → polished version in one prompt
  • Deck storylines → slide-by-slide outline generated instantly

Try this on your next project: Start your next research task by asking Copilot for a structured first draft. You'll spend your time refining instead of starting from scratch.

Quality

Copilot doesn't just make things faster — it can help you produce clearer, better-structured output. We've found it helpful when you need to:

  • Sharpen slide headlines from descriptive to action-oriented
  • Structure executive summaries using situation-complication-resolution
  • Catch gaps in your analysis by asking "what am I missing?"
  • Improve email clarity and tone for senior audiences

Try this on your next project: After writing a draft, paste it into Copilot and ask: "How can I make this more concise and impactful for a C-suite audience?"

Capability Expansion

Copilot lets you explore options and directions you might not have considered. It's not replacing your judgment — it's expanding your toolkit.

  • Generate 5–7 testable hypotheses to stress-test your thinking
  • Create competitive maps with positioning frameworks
  • Brainstorm strategic options with structured pros/cons
  • Draft interview discussion guides for unfamiliar industries

Try this on your next project: Before your next brainstorm, generate a set of hypotheses with Copilot. Use them as a starting point for the team conversation.

A Few Things to Keep in Mind

  • Always verify. Copilot is a starting point, not a final source. Cross-check numbers, quotes, and company details before they go to a client.
  • Iterate. The first prompt rarely gives you the best result. Refine your ask, add context, and have a conversation with Copilot to get better output.
  • Be mindful of confidentiality. Don't paste client-sensitive data into Copilot web mode. Use Copilot in M365 apps (Word, Excel, Teams) for client content.
  • Share what works. When you find a prompt or workflow that saves time, share it with the team via the Feedback Hub.
