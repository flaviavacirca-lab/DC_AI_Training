Getting Started with Copilot

1

Access Copilot

Open Microsoft 365 Copilot from the sidebar in Teams, Outlook, Word, or Excel. You can also visit copilot.microsoft.com in your browser.

2

Be Specific

Tell Copilot your role, the task, the context, and the format you want. The more detail, the better the output.

3

Iterate

Treat Copilot like a colleague — give feedback on its output, ask it to refine, and build on what it gives you.

4

Verify

Always review AI-generated content for accuracy, especially data points, client names, and strategic recommendations.
