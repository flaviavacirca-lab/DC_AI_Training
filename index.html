Garmin Connect Integration

Export workouts as TCX files to import into Garmin Connect. Your Garmin Forerunner 55 will sync structured workouts automatically.

How to Import

  1. Click "Export" on any workout or week
  2. Open Garmin Connect on your computer
  3. Go to Training → Workouts
  4. Click Import and select the downloaded .tcx file
  5. The workout will sync to your Forerunner 55
