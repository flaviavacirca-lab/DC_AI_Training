Accelerate Market Research

Get a structured market brief in minutes instead of hours.

The scenario: You're kicking off a new engagement and need to quickly size a market and identify key players. Normally you'd spend 2–3 hours pulling data from multiple sources and assembling it into a brief. With Copilot, you can get a solid first pass in 15 minutes.

Weak vs. Improved Prompt

Weak Prompt
"Tell me about the healthcare market."
You'll get a generic overview — think Wikipedia article. No structure, no sizing, no competitive landscape. Not useful for a client engagement.
Improved Prompt
"I'm a strategy consultant preparing for a client engagement in the U.S. digital health market. Help me create a market sizing framework that includes: (1) TAM/SAM/SOM estimates with sources, (2) key segments by buyer type, (3) top 10 companies by revenue, and (4) growth drivers for the next 3–5 years. Format as a structured brief I can share with my team."
You'll get an organized brief with clear sections, specific companies, estimated market sizes, and a framework your team can build on.

Why the improved prompt works

  • Context: It tells Copilot who you are and why you need this.
  • Specificity: It lists exactly what you want (TAM/SAM, companies, growth drivers).
  • Format: It asks for a structured brief, not a free-form essay.
  • Audience: It mentions sharing with the team, which shapes the tone.

Try This on Your Next Project

I'm a strategy consultant preparing for a client engagement in [INDUSTRY/MARKET]. Help me create a market sizing framework that includes:
1. TAM/SAM/SOM estimates with sources
2. Key segments by [BUYER TYPE / GEOGRAPHY / PRODUCT]
3. Top [N] companies by revenue or market share
4. Growth drivers for the next 3-5 years
Format as a structured brief I can share with my team.

Replace the bracketed sections with your specific engagement details. Use Copilot in web mode for broad research.
