Suggested Training Flow

Work through these in order, or jump to whatever fits your current needs.

Phase 1: Foundation

Get comfortable with Copilot before applying it to client work.

1

Copilot 101: The Basics

15 min

Where to find Copilot, the Web vs. Work toggle, how chat memory works, and your first 5 minutes.
2

Prompt Engineering (CRAFT Framework)

20 min

Learn the CRAFT framework for writing effective prompts. Includes interactive practice coach.
3

LinkedIn Learning: Copilot for Productivity

Self-paced

Microsoft & LinkedIn's official Copilot productivity path. Covers core features across M365 apps.

Phase 2: Core Consulting Workflows

Apply Copilot to the tasks you do every week on client engagements.

4

Research & Market Sizing

10 min

Size markets, map competitors, and build research briefs faster with structured prompts.
5

Interview Synthesis (Phase 0)

10 min

Turn interview notes into themes, contradictions, and presentation-ready quotes.
6

Deck Drafting & Storylining

10 min

Generate slide-by-slide storylines with action headlines and visual suggestions.
7

Market Mapping & Hypothesis Development

10 min

Create competitive landscapes and generate testable hypotheses in one prompt.
8

Data Interpretation & "So What" Statements

10 min

Find the story in data and draft actionable insights for client presentations.
9

Using Copilot Transcripts in Meetings

10 min

When to enable transcripts, post-meeting actions, and prompts to extract value.

Phase 3: Advanced

Level up once you're comfortable with the core workflows.

10

Copilot 102: Agents & Prompt Refinement

20 min

Pre-built agents, when to use them, and techniques for refining prompts iteratively.
11

Build Reusable Prompt Systems

25 min

Build a custom Copilot agent in Copilot Studio and create reusable prompt workflows.
12

Competitive Analysis with Copilot Researcher

20 min

Build structured competitor profiles, positioning maps, and strategic insights using the Researcher agent.
